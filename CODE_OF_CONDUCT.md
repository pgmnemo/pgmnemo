# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want `pgmnemo` to be a precise, technically serious, and respectful open-source project.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- asking questions in good faith
- separating critique of ideas from critique of people
- documenting evidence when disagreeing
- helping new contributors understand project expectations

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- deliberately hostile or inflammatory language
- bad-faith review behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- repeatedly derailing discussions away from technical substance

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, PRs, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Severe or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately using the reporting path documented in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
